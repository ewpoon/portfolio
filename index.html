Hello!


My name's Erica Poon and I am a multimedia artist with a passion for UX Research and Design.


I am currently a student at the University of Michigan pursuing a Bachelor's in Science in Information through the School of Information, as well as a Bachelor's in Arts in Art and Design through Penny Stamps School of Art and Design. I enjoy watching animations when I have free time, love to visit museums and galleries, and have an unhealthy obsession with Animal Crossing.


Currently, I am working as an intern on a research project at the School of Information in order to tackle transportation needs in Detroit. Alongside this, I am working with a team of UMSI Masters students in order to help redesign and rebrand a mental health care app for college students. I hope to be able to use the knowledge that I have gained throughout all of my experiences and put them to good use!
